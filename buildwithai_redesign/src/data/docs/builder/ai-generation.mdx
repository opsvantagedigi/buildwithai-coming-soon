# AI Generation Overview

The AI engine creates your site using structured prompts, templates, and your business description.

## What AI Generates

- Hero section
- About section
- Services
- Testimonials (optional)
- Contact section
- Navigation + footer
- Additional pages based on your sitemap

## Regeneration

You can regenerate:

- Individual sections
- Entire pages
- Branding tone
- Layout structure
